What is WYCF! WYCF Stands for WINNERS YOUTH CORPERS FELLOWSHIP. It is the community of corpers across states with the same fire to see revivial in the life of corpers WYCF exist in every state and is headed by the state coordinator. If you would like WYCF to come to your state you can reach out to us and we will be there in no time.
