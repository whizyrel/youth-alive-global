What is YAF! YAF Stands for YOUTH ALIVE FELLOWSHIP. It is the flagship community to gather youths and nurture the next generation of Kingdom giants YAF exist in all our church locations around the globe and in headed by the resident youth coordinator.
Why you should be part of us Welcome to the Winners Youth Alive! We are a vibrant community committed to nurturing young hearts and minds in faith. Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive space where young individuals can explore their spirituality, build meaningful connections, and grow into compassionate leaders. Our dedicated team of pastors, mentors, and volunteers work tirelessly to organize engaging events, weekly gatherings, service projects, and educational programs. We believe in fostering a culture of respect, understanding, and support.
